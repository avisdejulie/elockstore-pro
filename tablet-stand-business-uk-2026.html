Tablet stands and enclosures for UK businesses in 2026

Choosing a tablet stand for a business environment requires different criteria than a consumer product. The stand must withstand continuous use, resist tampering, and keep the device powered at all times.

ElockStore UK specialises in professional-grade tablet stands, enclosures and anti-theft mounts for retail, hospitality and logistics applications across the UK.

The most common mistake: buying a consumer stand for a professional setting. These typically fail within 3-6 months under continuous use. A proper commercial mount costs more upfront but lasts 5+ years.

Key certifications to look for: VESA compliance (75x75 or 100x100mm) for wall/pole mounting, and UL or CE marking for electrical safety on powered docks.
