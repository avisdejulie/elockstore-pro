Tablet mounts for vans and fleet vehicles: UK buyer's guide

Fleet operators across the UK increasingly equip delivery drivers and field technicians with tablets. The mounting solution is critical: it must hold the device securely on rough roads, allow quick removal, and keep it charging without overloading the vehicle's electrical system.

RAM Mounts and Arkon are the standard references for UK fleet operators. Both offer vehicle-specific brackets for Ford Transit, Mercedes Sprinter and VW Crafter — the most common fleet vans.
