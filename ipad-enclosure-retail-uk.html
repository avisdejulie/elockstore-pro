iPad enclosures for UK retail: what works and what doesn't

In UK retail, iPad enclosures serve two purposes: security (preventing theft) and presentation (keeping the device oriented and powered for customer interaction). Most budget enclosures do neither well.

Commercial-grade iPad enclosures available from ElockStore UK include key-locked housing, 360° rotation, and cable management — essential for busy retail floors.

For point-of-sale or self-service kiosks, the enclosure must also allow easy card reader attachment and barcode scanner integration. Verify compatibility before ordering.
